The new
Art Deco
era

The search for a new expression of the timeless style led to the birth of ERA. The first district in city to embody the deep essence of Art Deco. Art de vivre in a place of art.

архитектура

A spirited addition to city's cityscape, the ERA towers are a perfect blend of classic Art Deco and contemporary architecture. Their stepped silhouette, illuminated arches, and rich materials —a combination of black granite and metallic accents — reflect the GAFA bureau’s modern take on a classic style.

Art Deco has captivated artists, writers, architects, and designers for over a century. Its vibrant aesthetic promises unforgettable experiences and continues to inspire with its theatrical flair and elegance.

«The fundamental idea behind ERA is the tradition of the high-rise architecture of the last century, which forms a unique, recognisable silhouette of the world's major cities ». «The fundamental idea behind ERA – is the tradition of the high-rise architecture of the last century, which forms a unique, recognisable silhouette of the world's major cities».
